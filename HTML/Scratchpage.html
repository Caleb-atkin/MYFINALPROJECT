My Purpose

With over 30 years of experience in the study and practice of family dynamics and relationship counseling, I have dedicated my career to understanding the complexities that shape our most important connections. Through my own journey of raising a family, I have gained firsthand insight into the challenges and emotional toll that family life can bring. Navigating the intricacies of communication, conflict resolution, and balancing the many roles within a family can be overwhelming, and I have witnessed how these issues can lead to long-term stress and disconnection. Driven by a deep empathy and a passion for helping others, I made it my life's mission to guide families and individuals toward healthier, more fulfilling relationships. I have committed myself to developing strategies that not only help families avoid common pitfalls but also empower them to thrive despite challenges. My work focuses on providing practical tools, emotional support, and evidence-based therapeutic methods that foster mutual understanding, respect, and lasting change. Through a collaborative approach, I work with families to strengthen their bonds, improve communication, and create positive, sustainable shifts in their dynamics.

Our Team

At our Family Therapy Team, we are dedicated to helping families navigate challenges and build stronger, healthier relationships. Our team consists of experienced and compassionate therapists, counselors, and specialists who work collaboratively to address the unique needs of each family. Whether you’re struggling with communication, conflict, or navigating life transitions, we offer a safe, supportive environment where every family member’s voice is heard and valued. We specialize in systemic therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), and emotion-focused therapy (EFT), ensuring that our strategies are personalized to your family’s needs.

Packages

Empowerment Package

Our Youth Empowerment Package is designed to support teenagers and young adults in building confidence, improving communication skills, and overcoming obstacles in their personal lives. Whether you're dealing with school stress, family conflict, or feeling disconnected, this package is focused on helping you thrive.

  • Therapy sessions tailored to your needs and goals
  • Therapy sessions with peers to share experiences and build support networks
  • Guidance for improving emotional regulation and coping with stress
  • Opportunities with our experienced team to guide you through tough moments
  • Practical advice on navigating relationships, identity, and self-esteem
Family Strength Package

This package is designed for families with young people who need help improving communication and resolving conflicts together. We focus on strengthening the entire family unit by teaching healthy conflict resolution, building trust, and enhancing understanding between parents and children.

  • Therapy sessions focusing on communication and conflict resolution
  • Parenting support to address specific issues with children or adolescents
  • Youth-focused individual therapy to address personal concerns or challenges
  • Tools for creating family goals and maintaining healthy boundaries
  • Family support activities designed to strengthen emotional bonds
Academic & Life Skills Package

Designed for young people navigating the transition from high school to college or early adulthood, this package focuses on building skills for success in both academics and personal life. We address the challenges of managing responsibilities, setting goals, and maintaining healthy relationships during this important stage of life.

  • Time management and study strategies for academic success
  • Career and life goal setting to help you envision and achieve your future
  • Stress management tools to balance academic, social, and personal demands
  • One-on-one coaching sessions to address specific challenges, like self-doubt, procrastination, or motivation
  • Support in maintaining a balanced lifestyle, including mental health, exercise, and social connections
